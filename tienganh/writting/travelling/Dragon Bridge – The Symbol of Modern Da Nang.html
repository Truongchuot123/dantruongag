    Have you ever seen a dragon on a bridge? In the heart of Da Nang, a busy and modern city in central Vietnam, there is a bridge shaped like a giant dragon. In Vietnamese culture, dragons are a sign of luck and strength. The bridge was built in 2009 and opened in 2013. It is 666 meters long and has six lanes for cars and motorbikes. At night, the bridge is full of colorful LED lights. It's a fun place for families, couples, and travelers. Many people watch the show from the street, but some choose a boat trip on the Han River. From the boat, you can see the whole dragon and enjoy the fire and water show. Some boats also offer dinner and music, which makes the trip more fun and relaxing. Dragon Bridge is not just a bridge. It's a special part of Da Nang. If you visit the city, don't miss this amazing show. Maybe even watch it from a boat!
